Welcome to our Hostel 2962 Garmisch-Partenkirchen. Our Hostel has newly renovated rooms and is located in the center of Garmisch-Partenkirchen only 250m from the main trainstation. Parking is available behind the Hostel.
We offer 23 rooms, an appartment and a family-room.The breakfast room has a flatscreen TV and an "all you can eat breakfast" buffet for only 6,00 € per person. We also have a TV room with soda´s and alcoholic beverages and an lounge area.

There is a ski-room,charcoal grill,table with chairs in the front of the Hostel.Cafes, bars and restaurant are in walking distance.
